Sight over Sight: Perception-Aware Reinforcement Learning for Efficient Robotic Inspection

Abstract

This work presents a novel approach to perception-aware reinforcement learning for efficient robotic inspection tasks. Our method combines advanced perception techniques with reinforcement learning to enable robots to perform efficient and thorough inspections of complex environments.

By integrating perception awareness into the learning process, our approach allows robots to make informed decisions about where to look and how to navigate during inspection tasks. This leads to more efficient coverage and better detection of anomalies or points of interest.

We demonstrate that our Sight over Sight methodology significantly improves inspection efficiency while maintaining high detection accuracy across various robotic platforms and inspection scenarios.

Methodology

Our perception-aware reinforcement learning approach combines state-of-the-art computer vision techniques with intelligent path planning to optimize robotic inspection tasks.

Key components include adaptive viewpoint selection, efficient coverage algorithms, and real-time perception feedback integration.

Results

Our approach demonstrates significant improvements in inspection efficiency and accuracy across multiple robotic platforms and inspection scenarios.

Detailed results and comparisons with baseline methods will be presented in the full paper.

Future Directions

Future work will explore extensions to multi-robot inspection scenarios and integration with advanced sensor modalities for enhanced perception capabilities.
